We have the following columns in the table :

-> Batsman_Name
-> Batsman_Order (1,2,3, etc)
-> Player_Type (Batsman, Bowler, All Rounder, etc)
-> Runs_Scored (Total runs scored till date)
-> Debut_Year 
-> City (Example Virat Kohli belongs to Delhi)

Example -> 
* Operation to read the entire table
* Operation on the specific player data and we can update the score of the particular player.
* Operation to calculate the total score of the particular player.
* Operation to check the particular player exists in the list or not with the exists operator.
* Operation on the player to check the player score is present or not with the null operator.
* Operation to compare the value of all the playyers score with the help of all operator.
